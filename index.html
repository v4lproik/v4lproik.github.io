Independent Engineering Notebook

From government offensive security to crypto infrastructure and company building.

I started my career at 20 as a professional hacker working for the French government. Since then I have moved through large-scale distributed data systems, cryptography and offensive security for public-sector clients, protocol and validator research in crypto, and finally company building from zero to 20 people with $13.4M raised. I still operate close to the metal: architecture, code, systems, and the people shipping them. I also work comfortably across languages: French, English, and Spanish at C1, Danish at B2, and Korean in progress.
